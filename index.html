The No Communication Theorem

Study about Quantum Entanglement. Can we communicate faster that the speed of light using quantum computing ?

Jupiter, Python, MPQP

School: UTBM

Team size: 3

Date: 2025

Course: Classical and Quantum Algorithm

In this project, we demonstrate both mathematically and experimentally, that quantum entanglement cannot be used to transmit information:

  • Prepared a maximally entangled Bell state using the MPQP quantum circuit simulator.
  • Measured Alice’s qubit in different bases (Z and X) to test for signaling effects.
  • Measured Bob’s qubit independently and analyzed the statistical outcomes.
  • Proved mathematically using reduced density matrices that Bob’s local state is unaffected by Alice’s choices.
  • Confirmed through simulation that Bob’s measurement results remain 50/50, regardless of Alice’s measurement basis.
